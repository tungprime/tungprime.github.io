Tung T. Nguyen

I am an incoming Postdoctoral Associate in Mathematics at Western University. My mentors are professor Jan Minac, professor Lyle Muller, and others.

I am interested in number theory; specifically zeta values, heights and Tamagawa numbers of motives, Galois theory. My recent interest includes random graph theory, and computational neuroscience.

I obtained my Ph.D. at the University of Chicago in December 2020. My advisor is Professor Kazuya Kato. My dissertation is available here.

Before coming to Chicago, I was an undergraduate student at Vietnam National University. I wrote my senior thesis under the supervision of Professor Ralph Greenberg.
