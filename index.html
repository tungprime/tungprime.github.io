Tung Nguyen

I am a mathematician. I am interested in number theory; specifically zeta values, Iwasawa theory, and Hida theory. My current interest is in heights and Tamagawa numbers of motives, Galois theory, and random graph theory.

I defended my dissertation at the University of Chicago in October 2020. My advisor is Professor Kazuya Kato. My dissertation is available here.

Before coming to Chicago, I was an undergraduate student at Vietnam National University. I wrote my senior thesis under the supervision of Professor Ralph Greenberg.

I am on the job market starting fall 2020.
