Problem of the month

This page contains the list of math problems that are proposed monthly at Elmhurst University. All are welcome to submit their answers.

How to submit?

Please submit your work to tung.nguyen@elmhurst.edu.

While you can submitt your hand-written work, it is encouraged that you type your solution using Latex, which is a standard tool for writing math equations. Please see here for a quick introduction to Latex.
